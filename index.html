My Favourite Math

One of my favorite identities in trigonometry is the Law of Sines, which states that for any triangle:

\[ \frac{a}{\sin(A)} = \frac{b}{\sin(B)} = \frac{c}{\sin(C)} \]

This fundamental law relates the sides of a triangle to the sines of their opposite angles, making it a powerful tool in geometry, navigation, and applied mathematics.
